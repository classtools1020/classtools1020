🔮 句型 1：How's the weather?

「天氣怎麼樣？」

結構：How + is + the + weather + ?

回答：It is + 形容詞 + today.

📚 句型 1 範例

A: How's the weather?

B: It's hot today. ☀️

B: It's rainy today. 🌧️

B: It's sunny today. ☀️

🔮 句型 2：It is ___ today.

「今天 ___ 」

📌 It is hot today.

📌 It is cold today.

📌 It is rainy today.

⚠️ 文法陷阱

rain = 動詞 (It rains.)

rainy = 形容詞 (It is rainy.)

snow = 動詞 (It snows.)

snowy = 形容詞 (It is snowy.)

口訣：be 動詞後面用形容詞（加 y）！

🎯 Practice 3 題目

(C) snow
